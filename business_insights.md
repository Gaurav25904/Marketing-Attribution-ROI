# Business Insights

## Objective

Analyze the dashboard to identify key marketing performance insights.

## Key Insights

1. LinkedIn is the highest-performing marketing channel, generating the highest revenue and conversions.

2. The LinkedIn_B2B campaign recorded the highest revenue among all campaigns.

3. The dashboard achieved a total revenue of ₹341,950 with 736 conversions.

4. The overall ROAS of 2.07% indicates a positive return on advertising spend.

5. Revenue and conversion performance varies across channels, highlighting opportunities for optimization.

6. The User Journey Analysis shows that some users drop off before conversion, indicating room for improving the customer journey.

## Conclusion

The dashboard provides valuable insights into marketing performance, helping identify high-performing channels and campaigns while highlighting areas for improvement.

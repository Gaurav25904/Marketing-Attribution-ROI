# KPI Validation Notes

## CPC (Cost Per Click)

Formula:
CPC = Ad Spend / Clicks

Sample Calculation:
CPC = 16,489,830 / 983,806 = 16.76

Result:
CPC = ₹16.76 per click

---

## CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)

Formula:
CAC = Ad Spend / Conversions

Sample Calculation:
CAC = 16,489,830 / 736 = 22,404.66

Result:
CAC = ₹22,404.66 per acquired customer

---

## ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)

Formula:
ROAS = Revenue / Ad Spend

Sample Calculation:
ROAS = 341,950 / 16,489,830 = 0.0207

Result:
ROAS = 0.0207 (2.07%)

---

## KPI Validation Summary

| KPI | Formula | Result |
|-----|----------|---------|
| CPC | Ad Spend ÷ Clicks | ₹16.76 |
| CAC | Ad Spend ÷ Conversions | ₹22,404.66 |
| ROAS | Revenue ÷ Ad Spend | 0.0207 (2.07%) |

## Conclusion

The CPC, CAC, and ROAS formulas were validated using the cleaned marketing dataset.
The calculated values match the expected KPI definitions and can be used for future dashboard development and performance analysis.

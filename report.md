 Marketing Attribution Analysis Project
 Objective
The goal of this project is to analyze marketing campaign performance and identify which channels contribute most to conversions and revenue.

---

Data Cleaning
- Removed missing values
- Removed duplicate records
- Standardized column names
- Verified data types

---

 Dataset Information
- Table Name: cleaned_marketing
- Schema: marketing_attribution

Columns:
- User_ID
- Timestamp
- UTM_Source
- Campaign
- Converted
- Revenue
- Ad_Spend
- Clicks
- Impressions

---

 Analysis Performed

1. First-Touch Attribution
Identifies the first interaction that led to conversion.

2. Campaign Performance
- Total Revenue per campaign
- Total Conversions per campaign
- Ad Spend comparison

---

 Key Metrics

- Total Revenue
- Total Conversions
- Total Ad Spend
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)

---

 Dashboard
A Power BI dashboard was created to visualize:
- Campaign performance
- Conversion distribution
- Revenue trends

---

 Insights

- Some campaigns generated high revenue with low spend
- Certain channels had higher conversion rates
- Optimization opportunities identified in underperforming campaigns

---

 Conclusion

Marketing attribution helps businesses allocate budget effectively and improve ROI by focusing on high-performing channels.
